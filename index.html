Zenitsu Agatsuma

Bio

Zenitsu Agatsuma is one of the main characters in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a cowardly and timid boy who becomes a demon slayer after being saved by Tanjiro Kamado.

Abilities

Zenitsu is known for his Thunder Breathing techniques and incredible speed. Despite his fearful nature, he exhibits remarkable skill when fighting demons.
